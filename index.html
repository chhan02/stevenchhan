About Me

Hey! I am Steven Chhan.
Yes with two H's.

I am the Product Designer at JNR Incorporated in Irvine, CA. I operate as the lead user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) designer for existing and future products. I also contribute to frontend work - utilizing ReactJS, TailwindCSS, and raw CSS/SCSS. 

I've worked with several Global 500 companies like UnitedHealth Group, Hyundai, BMW, Toshiba and much more. My role is to sketch, prototype, and optimize UX while following corporate design guidelines. After the design process is complete, the team and I implement the work on the frontend. 

Outside of the work, I love traveling, photography, automotive, technology, sports, and - last but not least - spending time with my dog, Misa.
